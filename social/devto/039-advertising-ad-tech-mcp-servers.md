**At a glance:** 30+ servers. Google official backing. Meta Ads has the most popular single server (631 stars). Multi-platform management emerging fast. One of the strongest MCP categories.

## Google Ads (8+ Servers)

| Server | Stars | Key Feature |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| [cohnen/mcp-google-ads](https://github.com/cohnen/mcp-google-ads) | 459 | Community favorite, GAQL queries, 5 tools |

| [google-marketing-solutions/google_ads_mcp](https://github.com/google-marketing-solutions/google_ads_mcp) | 130 | Google Marketing Solutions team |

The deepest ecosystem in ad-tech MCP. The official server is intentionally minimal (read-only GAQL) — a safety feature for production accounts. Community servers fill the gap with campaign management, keyword research, and full CRUD.

## Meta / Facebook Ads

**[pipeboard-co/meta-ads-mcp](https://github.com/pipeboard-co/meta-ads-mcp)** (631 stars, 25 tools) — the **highest-starred advertising MCP server**. Full CRUD for campaigns, ad sets, ads, creatives. Targeting search, AI-powered analysis. Covers the entire Facebook/Instagram ad lifecycle.

**[gomarble-ai/facebook-ads-mcp-server](https://github.com/gomarble-ai/facebook-ads-mcp-server)** (254 stars, 23 tools) — unique activity/change history tracking for auditing campaign modifications.

## TikTok Ads

**[AdsMCP/tiktok-ads-mcp-server](https://github.com/AdsMCP/tiktok-ads-mcp-server)** (23 stars, 10 tools) — campaign management and analytics. **[ysntony/tiktok-ads-mcp](https://github.com/ysntony/tiktok-ads-mcp)** (18 stars, 6 tools) — read-only safety-first, multi-advertiser support.

## Multi-Platform Management

**[amekala/ads-mcp](https://github.com/amekala/ads-mcp)** (19 stars, 100+ tools) — Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok. Strategy persistence via STRATEGY.md across sessions. Strongest LinkedIn Ads coverage (28 tools).

**[jshorwitz/synter-mcp-server](https://github.com/jshorwitz/synter-mcp-server)** (8 stars, 140+ tools) — **9 platforms** including Reddit, StackAdapt, The Trade Desk. AI creative generation (Imagen 4, Flux, Veo).

## Protocol: Ad Context Protocol (AdCP)

**[adcontextprotocol/adcp](https://github.com/adcontextprotocol/adcp)** (193 stars, Apache-2.0) — open standard for AI-driven ad operations across 8 domains. Built on MCP + A2A. Signals industry maturation.

## Ad Auditing

**[AgriciDaniel/claude-ads](https://github.com/AgriciDaniel/claude-ads)** (981 stars) — 190+ audit checks across 7 ad platforms with parallel agent analysis. Claude Code skill, not MCP server. Highest-starred project in the space.

## Notable Gaps

No dedicated Amazon Ads server (beta only). No Pinterest or Snapchat Ads. No cross-platform attribution. No ad fraud detection. No retail media networks.

## Rating: 4.5/5

One of the strongest MCP categories. Google's official involvement signals enterprise readiness. Meta Ads has the best single server. Multi-platform solutions are the most exciting development. Half-point off for missing Amazon Ads and attribution tools.
