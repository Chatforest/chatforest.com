**At a glance:** One of the most culturally diverse MCP categories. 20+ servers across 8 traditions — BaZi, Western astrology, tarot, I Ching, horoscopes, and commercial platforms. **Rating: 3.5/5.**

## BaZi / Chinese Astrology

**cantian-ai/bazi-mcp** (271 stars, TypeScript) — Highest-starred server by far. Computes the Four Pillars of Destiny (八字): heavenly stems, earthly branches, Five Elements, Ten Gods, hidden stems, major life phases, compatibility. Solar and lunar datetime support. Derived from a popular GPT Store app.

**AngusHsu/lunar-mcp-server** (Python, MIT, 20 tools) — Broader Chinese calendar: auspicious dating, festival info, moon phases, calendar conversion, BaZi, zodiac insights. Location-aware with timezone support.

## Western Astrology

**simpolism/AstroMCP** (14 stars, TypeScript) — Natal charts via Swiss Ephemeris. **dm0lz/swiss-ephemeris-mcp-server** (7 stars, MIT) — Pure astronomical calculations: planetary positions, transits, synastry, solar returns.

## Ephemeris

**ascorbic/ephemeris** (8 stars, 11 tools, GPL-3.0) — Free hosted remote MCP at **ephemeris.fyi**. No installation needed — just connect to the URL. The easiest way to add astrological calculations to any MCP client.

## Tarot

**fzlzjerry/tarot-mcp** (7 stars, 8 tools) — Most feature-rich: 11 professional spreads, custom spread creation, elemental analysis, **cryptographically secure shuffling**. **abdul-hamid-achik/tarot-mcp** (6 stars, MIT, 9 tools, 10 spreads). **junebash/swift-tarot-mcp** (Swift, MIT).

## I Ching

**threemachines/i-ching** (10 stars, Rust, MIT) — The most culturally faithful implementation. Uses the authoritative **Wilhelm-Baynes translation** with all 64 hexagrams, judgments, images, and line interpretations. **MCP-Liuyao** adds Liu Yao six-line divination with true solar time calculation.

## Horoscopes & Commercial

**GBcui/horoscope-serve** — Daily/weekly/monthly horoscopes for all signs. **Astrology-API.io** — 16 tools, 23 house systems, 97+ Arabic parts, free tier. **RoxyAPI** — 110+ endpoints across astrology, tarot, numerology, I Ching, crystals, dream interpretation, angel numbers.

## What's Missing

- No standalone numerology MCP server
- No rune divination
- No feng shui calculator
- No palmistry or face reading tools

**Rating: 3.5/5** — Diverse traditions represented with genuine calculation depth. BaZi dominates by community interest, Western astrology has solid Swiss Ephemeris foundations, tarot has proper randomization, and I Ching has a culturally faithful Rust implementation. Most servers are individual projects — the commercial tier is where the real feature density lives.
