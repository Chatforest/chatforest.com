**At a glance:** 40+ MCP servers across genomics, proteomics, drug discovery, clinical trials, medical imaging, and healthcare interoperability. Integrated platforms lead: biomcp (241 stars) and Anthropic's life-sciences marketplace (259 stars). **Rating: 4/5.**

## Integrated Platforms

### biomcp (241 stars)
The most popular standalone biomedical MCP — a single-binary CLI and MCP server querying ClinicalTrials.gov, PubMed, genomic variants, and more. Python, MIT licensed, 12 entity types.

### Anthropic life-sciences (259 stars)
Official Anthropic marketplace with PubMed, BioRender, 10x Genomics skills. The largest curated set of biomedical MCP integrations.

## Literature & Clinical Research

**PubMed:** JackKuo666/PubMed-MCP-Server (105 stars — search, PDF download, deep analysis) and cyanheads/pubmed-mcp-server (66 stars — NCBI E-utilities, multi-format citations).

**Clinical trials:** Cicatriiz/healthcare-mcp-public (102 stars — 9 tools spanning FDA, PubMed, ClinicalTrials, ICD-10, DICOM) and cyanheads/clinicaltrialsgov-mcp-server (59 stars — patient matching, trend analysis).

## Sequence Analysis & Systems Biology

- **bio-mcp organization** — BLAST, BWA, SeqKit, AMBER, InterPro servers
- **longevity-genie/gget-mcp** (27 stars) — ~15 tools wrapping gget library, BLAST, AlphaFold
- **Reactome, OpenTargets, BioThings** servers for pathway analysis and gene-drug-disease associations

## Healthcare Interoperability

- **jmandel/health-record-mcp** — SMART on FHIR EHR access
- **wso2/fhir-mcp-server** — expose any FHIR server as MCP
- **Cicatriiz healthcare-mcp-public** — DICOM metadata

## Gaps

No direct AlphaFold MCP, no Galaxy workflow wrapper, limited single-cell pipeline support, no KEGG/Reactome pathway visualization, minimal medical imaging.

**Rating: 4/5** — One of the deepest scientific MCP categories. Protein databases, literature search, and clinical trials are well-covered. Integrated platforms (biomcp, Anthropic marketplace) make entry easy. Gaps in imaging and workflow tools keep it from 5/5.
