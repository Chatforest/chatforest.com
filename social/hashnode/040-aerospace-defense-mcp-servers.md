**At a glance:** 35+ servers. NASA data, orbital mechanics, aviation, drones, satellites, earth observation. Defense entirely absent.

## Space & Orbital Mechanics

**[NASA-MCP-server](https://github.com/ProgramComputer/NASA-MCP-server)** (80 stars) — 20+ NASA APIs: APOD, Mars Rovers, NEO, Space Weather, EPIC, EONET.

**[IO-Aerospace MCP](https://github.com/IO-Aerospace-software-engineering/mcp-server)** — production-hosted SPICE astrodynamics at mcp.io-aerospace.org. No install needed.

**[stk-mcp](https://github.com/alti3/stk-mcp)** (20 stars) — Ansys STK bridge for satellite mission engineering.

## Aviation

**[aerospace-mcp](https://github.com/cheesejaguar/aerospace-mcp)** — 44+ tools, 28,000+ airports, flight planning + orbital mechanics. **[aviation-mcp](https://github.com/blevinstein/aviation-mcp)** — FAA weather APIs, charts.

## Drones & Satellites

**[MAVLinkMCP](https://github.com/ion-g-ion/MAVLinkMCP)** — natural language drone control via MAVLink (PX4/ArduPilot). **[N2YO-MCP](https://github.com/MaxwellCalkin/N2YO-MCP)** — satellite tracking, space debris. **[Axion](https://github.com/Dhenenjay/axion-planetary-mcp)** (190 stars) — Google Earth Engine imagery.

## What's Good

SPICE-powered orbital mechanics. NASA's 20+ API gateway. 44-tool aviation suite. MAVLink universal drone protocol.

## What's Not

No defense servers. No official space agency servers. No FlightAware/FlightRadar24. No CFD or FEA integration.

## Rating: 3.5/5

Strong in specific niches (orbital mechanics, NASA data, aviation, drones) but defense entirely absent and coverage fragmented.
