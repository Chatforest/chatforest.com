**At a glance:** Two official vendor servers (Xero, QuickBooks) plus Sage. Strong plain-text accounting. One of the most production-ready MCP verticals.

## Official Vendor Servers

**[XeroAPI/xero-mcp-server](https://github.com/XeroAPI/xero-mcp-server)** (207 stars, 50+ tools) — contacts, invoices, payroll, reporting. 90 commits, 19 contributors.

**[intuit/quickbooks-online-mcp-server](https://github.com/intuit/quickbooks-online-mcp-server)** (108 stars, 11 entity types) — full CRUD for accounts, bills, customers, invoices, and more.

**Sage Intacct** — official server via Developer Portal on Sage AI Gateway.

## Community Highlights

**[kkeeling/zoho-mcp](https://github.com/kkeeling/zoho-mcp)** (37 stars, 20+ tools) — Zoho Books with 7-region support. **[laf-rge/quickbooks-mcp](https://github.com/laf-rge/quickbooks-mcp)** (30+ tools) — draft-by-default safety model. **[vinnividivicci/wave_mcp](https://github.com/vinnividivicci/wave_mcp)** (9 stars) — free Wave Accounting integration.

## Plain-Text Accounting

**[mcp-server-ledger](https://github.com/minhyeoky/mcp-server-ledger)** (45 stars, 9 tools) — Ledger CLI. **[beanquery-mcp](https://github.com/vanto/beanquery-mcp)** (41 stars) — BQL queries against Beancount.

## What's Good

Two official vendor servers. Strong plain-text accounting. Draft-by-default safety in community QuickBooks. Multiple Zoho implementations including file upload support.

## What's Not

No tax preparation. No bank feed integration. No expense management platforms. No audit trail or SOC 2 logging.

## Rating: 4.0/5

Production-ready. Official backing from Xero, QuickBooks, and Sage makes accounting one of the few MCP verticals with enterprise vendor buy-in.
